## Key Features of New Events

### 1. Diverse Themes
Each event has a completely unique visual identity with custom color schemes, particle effects, and special targets.

### 2. Balanced Challenge Tiers
- Bronze/Silver: Accessible to casual players
- Gold: Moderate challenge for regular players
- Platinum: Difficult for dedicated players
- Diamond: Elite challenges for hardcore players

### 3. Score Multipliers
Events feature varied score multipliers (1.3x to 2.5x) to encourage participation and make progression feel rewarding.

### 4. Unique Modifiers
Some events include special gameplay modifications:
- Speed changes (Cosmic Voyage: 0.8x, Cyber Week: 1.2x)
- Multiple particle effects
- Special target types

### 5. Recurring Rewards
Many skins and themes are marked as "recurring" - they'll return in future years with updated season numbers (e.g., "2025", "2026").

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Diversity
Every event has a distinct color palette and particle system, ensuring no two events feel similar.

### Reward Value
Higher tier rewards feature:
- More intense glow effects
- Unique animations
- Prestigious titles
- Collectible badges

### Challenge Balance
Requirements are tuned to feel achievable yet rewarding:
- Score challenges: 77,777 to 250,000 points
- Combo challenges: 10x to 150x
- Target challenges: 50 to 3,000 hits
- Game challenges: 3 to 100 games

### Accessibility
- Multiple difficulty tiers accommodate all skill levels
- Progress persists across sessions
- No pay-to-win mechanics
- Equal opportunity for all players
