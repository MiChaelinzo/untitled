### What is it?
Every game you play (except practice mode) is automatically recorded. You can watch replays to analyze your performance and find areas to improve.

### How to access:
1. Click the **"Replays"** tab in the main menu
2. You'll see a grid of all your recorded games
3. Each card shows: Score, Accuracy, Max Combo, Difficulty, and Date

### Watching a replay:
1. Click **"Watch Replay"** on any game card
2. Use the playback controls at the bottom:

   - **Speed button** - Toggle between 0.5x, 1x, and 2x speed
   - **Rewind/Forward buttons** - Skip backward or forward 5 seconds
   - **Timeline slider** - Drag to jump to any point in the game

### What you'll see:
- **Main View**: Watch targets appear and see your hits in real-time
- **Performance Summary**: Your accuracy %, max combo, and average reaction time

### Managing replays:
- **Search**: Type player name or difficulty to filter

- **Delete**: Click trash icon to remove unwanted replays
- **Storage info**: See total replay count and storage used at the top

## â“ FAQs

**Q: How much storage do replays use?**  
A: Each replay is about 50-100KB. With 50 replays, you're using ~5MB of storage.

**Q: Can I share replays with others?**  
A: Not yet, but this is a planned feature for a future update!

**Q: Do practice games get recorded?**  
A: No, only ranked games are recorded to keep your replay library meaningful.

**Q: Can I see who reacted to community posts?**  
A: Currently you see reaction counts. Individual reactor visibility may come in a future update.

**Q: Why don't I see my activities in the community feed?**  
A: Activities post automatically after certain achievements. Play games and complete challenges to appear!

**Q: How long are activities shown in the feed?**  
A: Activities remain indefinitely, but Trending only shows last 24 hours.
